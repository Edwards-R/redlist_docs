## Provisional Red List status: RE

## Red List Justification
*N/A*

### Narrative
The sole British record, the type species of this megachilid bee, was a female taken by Kirby at Coddenham, East Suffolk, prior to 1802, though there is no firm evidence to suggest the species was a vagrant or accidental introduction. Globally, the species is known from France and Spain in the west to China in the east but must be considered RE in a GB context.
