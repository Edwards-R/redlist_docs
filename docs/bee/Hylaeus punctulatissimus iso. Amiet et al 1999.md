# Hylaeus punctulatissimus: iso. Amiet et al: 1999

## Provisional Red List status: NE

## Red List Justification
Based on a single record from 1850 in London. Considered a probable misidentification.
### Narrative
The presence of this as a UK species is based entirely on 19th Century records for which collecting localities cannot be ascertained and include at least one misidentification. For this reason, the species has not been evaluated.
