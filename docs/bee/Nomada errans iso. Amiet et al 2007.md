### Narrative
Through the timespan of records (1878 â€“ 1982) this has always been a scarce and very restricted parasitic bee (1 small area of coast on the Isle of Purbeck, Dorset). Despite the increasing range of its host species (Andrena nitidiuscula) and a great deal of targeted searching under the Biodiversity Action Plan (~ 1995-2010) it has not been found in the assessment period and is evaluated as RE. Parasitic species often become extinct a long time before a host shows evidence of considerable and serious decline.
