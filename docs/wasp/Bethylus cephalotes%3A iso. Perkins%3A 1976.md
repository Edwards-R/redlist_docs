### Narrative


The most threatening accepted population decline was inferred at 0% using expert inference (A2). This does not exceed the 30% decline required for qualification as VU. The EoO (259,300 km^2) exceeds the 20,000 km^2 VU threshold for criterion B1 and does not satisfy sufficient subcriteria to reach a threat status, and the AoO (336 km^2) is below the 500 km^2 EN threshold for criterion B2 but does not satisfy sufficient subcriteria to reach a threat status. For Criterion D2, the number of locations was greater than 5 and there is no plausible threat that could drive the taxon to CR or RE in a very short time. No information was available on population size to inform assessments against Criteria C and D1; nor were any life-history models available to inform an assessment against Criterion E.
