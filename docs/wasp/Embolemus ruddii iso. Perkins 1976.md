    # Embolemus ruddii: iso. Perkins: 1976

## Provisional Red List status: DD
- A2 b,c
- A3 b
- D2

## Red List Justification
*N/A*
### Narrative
This is the only species in it's genus in GB. As with other bethyloid wasps, almost nothing is known about the species in terms of biology and ecology. The verified records for South West England, Norfolk, the Welsh borders and the East Midlands are highly unlikely to reflect the true distribution of the species. 

Insufficient data were available to reach a supportable conclusion on the threat status for this taxon.
