### Narrative
This parasitic wasp has been recorded very infrequently. Last known in Herefordshire in 1983, which has been re-checked 2022 and found to be genuine, but identification difficulties and an apparent elusiveness on the part of the wasp hampers any elucidation of biology or ecology.

Insufficient data were available to reach a supportable conclusion on the threat status for this taxon.
